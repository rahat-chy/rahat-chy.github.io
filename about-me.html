About Me

Hi, I am Rahat Chowdhury and I am from Dhaka, Bangladesh. I am currently working as a Software Engineer at Enosis Solutions, and I have done my undergrad at the Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) department of Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (AUST) with CGPA 3.807 out of 4.00. Prior to that, I completed my SSC from Dhanmondi Govt. Boys' High School and completedm my HSC from Birshreshtha Noor Mohammad Public College.

I have a huge enthusiasm for solving problems and have been doing competitive programming since the beginning of my undergraduate life. I have taken part in a number of intra-university and inter-university programming contests and also participated in 2022 ICPC Dhaka Regional onsite contest. In addition, I have solved a good number of problems in online judges like Leetcode, Codeforces, Codechef, UVA, etc. and participated in many contests in those online judges.

Outside of programming, I enjoy going to gym and workout, which helps me maintain a well-rounded lifestyle and fuels my creativity.
